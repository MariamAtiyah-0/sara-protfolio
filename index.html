About Me

Design that

I'm Sara Atiyah, a graphic designer based in Egypt with a foundation in Applied Arts — Interior Design. I build cohesive visual identities and advertising campaigns that give brands a distinct, memorable presence.

Working fluently across Arabic and English, I approach every project — from full brand systems to product campaigns and social content — with cultural precision and a sharp eye for detail.

Backed by two specialized training programs in graphic and creative design, I've translated that foundation into real client work, delivering brand systems and campaigns end-to-end for businesses like VeTiva and Seven Plus.
